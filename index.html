About ICPL

A professional cricket platform in Italy

ICPL is created to bring together competitive cricket, passionate supporters, businesses and community leaders. The tournament gives players a professional stage and gives sponsors a powerful opportunity to reach cricket-loving audiences across Italy and abroad.

With strong visibility among Pakistani, Indian, Bangladeshi, Sri Lankan and international communities, ICPL is more than a tournament — it is a sports and business platform.
